Key Features

Class Schedule Organizer

Keep track of all your classes and academic events in one place for easy management.

Set Reminders and Alarms

Never miss an important deadline again! Set reminders and alarms to stay on top of your tasks.

Organize Study Materials

Manage study materials for each course in one place and access them easily when needed.

About

Student Companion App is a project inspired by my personal experience as a student who struggled to keep track of classes, assignments, and study materials. As a result of this struggle, I decided to create an app that could simplify the student life by providing a user-friendly interface to manage academic tasks efficiently. This is my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I've had the opportunity to enhance my skills in app development and showcase my passion for creating useful applications.
